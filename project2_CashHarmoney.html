Cash HarMoney

Responsive Web APP Case study

Role: Product Research | UX Analysis | UX Design | Name Development | Brand Design | UI Design

Guiding Principles:

We want to encourage users to be aware of their debt reductions, saving goals, mortgages, retirement, and spending budgets in order to stay away from debt forever! To be successful, the individual’s cash balance is the key factor in achieving these milestones. We want to portray wisdom and knowledge for all things finance.

Tagline:

    Balancing your budget has never been easier.

Key Messaging:

    • “A finance app you can trust.”
    • “Anyone can save money!”
    • “It’s like having a financial advisor in your pocket!"

user stories

    • “As a user, I want to see an overview of how much my finances have changed and how much I am saving throughout the saving period, so that I can stay on track.”
    • “As a money-saver, I need to be able to input information on the money I am receiving and spending (and on what), so that I can see an overview of my finances.”
    • “As a user, I want to see a dashboard of my finances clearly and visually, so that I can see how much I am spending on what at a glance.”

Next iteration:

    Expand on offering various visual graphs for users to choose from when setting up a savings or debt reduction goal. Build out options to connect with users bank account.
